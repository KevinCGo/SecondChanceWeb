Our Story



Color Technologies was founded on July 2021 and is located in Milwaukee, WI. The company is co-owned by 2 members, Kevin Go and Xeleceem Xiong.They are both gradudates of i.c.stars, a technology based program, ever since November 2021. What began as a fictional concept during i.c.stars has now become a reality. While the company is fairly new, Color Tech is partnering with American Family Insurance as well as American Family Insurance Institute for corporate and social impact, to fight against the disadvantages of incarcerated individuals. Color Tech sees how the world is evolving interactively with technology and wants to partake in that.


At Color Technologies we believe in bringing ingenuity and imagination to businesses by understanding the customer's mission and providing creative web design solutions. Color Technologies strives to bring representation of minorities into technology.
